AQI Classification (OpenWeatherMap Scale)
Index Category PM2.5 (µg/m³) Health Impact Indicator
1 Good 0 – 10 Air quality is satisfactory. Little to no risk. Good
2 Fair 10 – 25 Acceptable. Possible concern for unusually sensitive groups. Fair
3 Moderate 25 – 50 Sensitive groups may experience health effects. Moderate
4 Poor 50 – 75 Everyone may begin to experience health effects. Poor
5 Very Poor 75+ Health warnings. Everyone may experience serious effects. Very Poor
